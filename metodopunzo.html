Metodo Punzo

Illustrated brochure

Metodo Punzo Running & Team Coaching is a company that wants to follow the life of runners to help them during their training sessions and recovery. The illustrated brochure tells the story of Marco and Sara, a runner and a trail runner. The freehand drawings and the texts in bubbles give a comics style to the brochure. Each memeber of the specialized team has a personalized character and they will guide and follow the athletes, step by step.
