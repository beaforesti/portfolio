Caffè Borbone

Website redesign

The project is the website redesign of Caffè Borbone Online e-commerce according to the principles of usability and ergonomics.

The first part focuses on the user research, the construction of personas, the analysis of the competitors and the parameters of usability. Then, we propose a redesign solution working on both graphical elements and architecture, and we prototype it.


My skills user research, UX, UI, prototype

Software Figma
