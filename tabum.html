taBÙM

Illustrated books

The project investigates the theme of death with a speculative approach. Starting from the identification of death as a taboo subject, we created taBÙM, a publishing house dedicated to childhood that tells about taboo subjects with frank naturalness.

The project includes the development of the series "Martino" whose first release is the picture book "La Maestra non respira" (The Teacher doesn't breathe). The illustrations have a style suitable for children, they are characterized by irregular shapes then shaded.
Illustration mentioned by Illustrators Virtual Wall of Bologna Children's Book Fair 2020.
