Every step you take

Infopoetry video

Infopoetry project to experiment with a poetical approach the representation of data.

Every day starts with some steps that generate data and digital traces. The stop-motion video tells the story of some students during a typical university day at the Design campus of Politecnico of Milan and it’s narrated from the point of view of the steps that they take. The footprints are the metaphor of the data we produce by simply walking. In this way we are constantly leaving traces of our passage.
